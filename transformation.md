# Transformation Projects

## Digital Workflow Automation
- **Project Name:** Billing Ticketing System  
- **Objective:** Creation of a new APEX tool to manage emails received by North America billing teams.  
- **Scope:** ~7 months; billing team of 35; involved areas: Billing NA, Customer Data Maintenance, AR, Global GPOs, Developers, etc.  
- **Role:** Lead supervisor connecting GPOs and the Billing team; managed end-to-end project activities, led implementation communication strategy, supervised and supported the business analyst through all project phases, approved testing results, and drove the rollout.  
- **Outcomes / Benefits:** Tool created; ~70% of email inquiries migrated to the system; tracking of email handling time implemented; successful implementation with an excellent communication strategy.  
- **Tools Used:** APEX, Outlook, Confluence, Microsoft Planner  

- **Project Name:** Credit Memo Mass Application  
- **Objective:** Automate the application of credit memos to original invoices.  
- **Scope:** ~10 months; involved areas: Accounts Receivable (AR) and Billing.  
- **Role:** Led as Business Analyst; created the Project Charter, coordinated discussions with the GPO for the Oracle team, produced the BRD outlining the process through development, coordinated testing with the Billing team, and oversaw implementation.  
- **Outcomes / Benefits:** ~70% of credit memos applied automatically by original invoice number; approximately 80% reduction in manual intervention in Oracle, reducing analyst workload and errors.  
- **Tools Used:** Oracle ERP, Confluence
