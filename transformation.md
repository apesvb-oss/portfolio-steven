# Transformation Projects

## Digital Workflow Automation
- **Context:** Manual approval processes were slowing down operations and causing delays.
- **Role:** Operations Supervisor
- **Actions:** Led the implementation of automated workflows using cloud-based tools, coordinated training sessions, and managed stakeholder communication.
- **Results:** Approval cycle time reduced by 60%, improved transparency, and increased employee satisfaction.

## Customer Support AI Bot Integration
- **Context:** Customer service teams faced high ticket volumes and repetitive inquiries.
- **Role:** Supervisor of Operations
- **Actions:** Oversaw the integration of an AI chatbot into the support platform, aligned workflows with IT, and ensured compliance with service standards.
- **Results:** Reduced response times by 40%, freed up human agents for complex cases, and improved customer satisfaction scores.
