# Project Management Model

## Methodologies & Frameworks

### Methodologies
- I work with **Scrum**, **Agile**, **Six Sigma**, and **PMI** methodologies to manage projects effectively and adapt to different organizational needs.

### ITIL Framework
- I apply **ITIL best practices** for incident, change, and service management to ensure operational stability, reliability, and alignment with business objectives.

---

## Project Phases & Documentation

### Project Phases
1. **Initiation**: Define objectives, scope, stakeholders, and feasibility.
2. **Planning**: Develop schedules, resource allocation, risk strategies, and communication plans.
3. **Execution**: Carry out project tasks, deliverables, and team coordination.
4. **Control (Monitoring)**: Track progress, manage risks, and ensure alignment with goals.
5. **Closing**: Formal project completion, deliverables acceptance, and lessons learned.
6. **Hypercare**: Post-deployment support phase to stabilize operations and address issues quickly.

### Key Documents
- **Project Charter**: Authorizes the project, outlines objectives, scope, and stakeholders.
- **Business Case**: Provides justification, cost-benefit analysis, and expected value.
- **Stakeholder Matrix**: Identifies stakeholders, their influence, and communication strategies.
- **Risk Matrix**: Maps risks by likelihood and impact, guiding mitigation plans.
- **Testing Documentation**: Includes test plans, test cases, and results to validate deliverables.
- **Mock-Up / mFile**: Prototype or visual representation to align expectations before development.
- **Close Form**: Official record of project closure, confirming deliverables and stakeholder approval.
- **Other Supporting Docs**: Communication plans, lessons learned, compliance checklists, and additional governance artifacts.

# PM Style & Leadership Style

## Project Management Style
My project management style is structured yet adaptable. I combine **Scrum, Agile, Six Sigma, PMI**, and **ITIL best practices** to ensure projects are delivered with efficiency, quality, and governance. I emphasize:
- Clear definition of scope, objectives, and deliverables.
- Strong documentation across all phases (charter, business case, risk matrix, stakeholder matrix, etc.).
- Continuous monitoring and control to keep projects aligned with business goals.
- A focus on operational stability during hypercare to guarantee long-term success.

This approach balances flexibility with discipline, ensuring both innovation and reliability in project execution.

## Leadership Style
My leadership style is collaborative and empowering. I focus on:
- **Servant Leadership**: Supporting the team by removing obstacles and enabling them to perform at their best.
- **Transparency & Communication**: Maintaining open channels with stakeholders and team members to build trust.
- **Mentorship & Growth**: Encouraging professional development and knowledge sharing within the team.
- **Decision-Making**: Using data-driven insights while remaining adaptable to changing circumstances.
- **Accountability**: Leading by example, fostering responsibility, and ensuring alignment with organizational values.

This style creates a culture of trust, ownership, and high performance, where teams feel motivated and projects achieve sustainable results.
