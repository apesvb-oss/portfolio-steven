# Project Management Model

My project management approach is based on combining Agile practices with ITIL principles to ensure efficiency, adaptability, and continuous improvement.

## Key Elements of My Model
- **Agile Methodologies:** I apply Scrum and Kanban to manage tasks, prioritize deliverables, and maintain flexibility in changing environments.
- **ITIL Framework:** I use ITIL best practices for incident, change, and service management to ensure stability and reliability in operations.
- **Risk Management:** I identify potential risks early, develop mitigation strategies, and monitor progress to minimize impact.
- **Stakeholder Engagement:** I maintain clear communication channels, provide regular updates, and align project goals with business objectives.
- **Continuous Improvement:** I promote feedback loops, retrospectives, and performance metrics to drive sustainable enhancements.

## My Leadership Style
I focus on empowering teams, fostering collaboration across departments, and ensuring accountability. My goal is to deliver measurable impact while building trust and resilience in every project.
