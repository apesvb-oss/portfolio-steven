# Operations Projects

# MediaTools Process Acquisition

## Objective
Migration of planning processes and tasks related to digital campaign planning to the CR operations team.

## Scope
- Duration: ~6 months  
- Team size: 9 analysts  
- Areas involved: Investment Media and Ops Media  

## Role
I led the planning of the migration. I learned the existing processes, developed training plans, trained the Ops team, and gradually transitioned campaign planning tasks to them—from campaign creation through to completion in the Actual phase. Additionally, I implemented process improvements, enhanced reporting, and designed a comprehensive operations strategy.

## Outcomes / Benefits
- New operations strategy for the Best Buy account  
- Significant reduction in reporting errors and turnaround times (nearly 80% improvement)  
- Faster resolution of data issues  
- Creation of 6 new positions in CR  
- More flexible processes that optimized client operations and strengthened PGD CR’s positioning  

## Tools Used
- MediaTools  
- Reporting dashboards created for each phase (Brief, Concept, Tactical, Actual)
